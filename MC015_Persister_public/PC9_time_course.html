3 QC and preprocess of individual samples

Basic steps:

  1. Create Seurat object from raw data.
  2. Quality control:
    1. Taste the threshold to filter low quality cells using genes per cell, percentage of reads mapped to mitochondrial genes and/or ribosomal protein genes. The genes present in very few cells are also excluded.
    2. Genes expressed at extremely high level, such as mitochondrial genes or ribosomal protein genes, are also excluded.
  3. Preprocessing following Seurat documentation, particularly vignettes Guided Clustering Tutorial and Cell-Cycle Scoring and Regression.
